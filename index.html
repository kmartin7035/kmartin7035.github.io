About Katherine Martin

Katherine Martin works in data architecture and experimental prose. She uses her background in chemistry for the construction of complex data systems and the maintenance of a single, oversized houseplant. Her work has appeared in Always Crashing, and she is the author of the novella Delete Me Kindly (Calamari Archive). She is currently at work on a novel and collection of short stories.
